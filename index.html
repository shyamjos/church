St. Antonys Church Cheriyappilly

Tuesday is the novena day in St. Antony’s Shrine, Kaloor. It is a very special day of devotion to St. Antony. People from all over Kerala travels to cochin to participate in the liturgical celebrations in the shrine. The thousands of devotees from early morning till the midnight surround the shrine and surroundings. Almost all the spiritual practices in the Shrine is mainly connected to Novena day.People are often seen in tears when they ask St. Antony for intercession in their needs and they really believe that St. Antony will help them.
